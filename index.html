Welcome to JamBox!

JamBox empowers you to analyze and visualize your music listening habits directly from your own device. We provide you with personalized insights into your top songs, recently played tracks, and a fascinating spread of graphs showcasing your unique music tastes, all without ever accessing or storing any of your personal information.

Your Data, Your Control

Our web application operates entirely on your laptop or device, meaning your data remains strictly within your control. We do not connect to any external servers of our own, ensuring that your music analysis experience is private and self-contained.

How It Works:

Simply connect your Spotify account to JamBox, and we will handle the rest. Our analysis takes place locally on your device, and all results are presented to you in real-time. There is no need to worry about your data being sent to external servers because it never leaves your device.

Key Features

  • Top Songs Over Time: Discover how your favorite tracks change over different time periods. Explore your musical journey and see how your taste evolves.
  • Recently Played: Stay up-to-date with your most recent musical adventures. Our interface will showcase your latest listens, keeping you in touch with your current music interests.
  • Graphs on Your Music Tastes: Delve into your music preferences through captivating graphs. Visualize genres, artists, and other musical insights that reveal the unique aspects of your musical taste.

Please not that since this app has been created purely as a hobby project, as per Spotify's Guidelines, it cannot be published out of development mode. Therefore, this application will only work for authorized users.
