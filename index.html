Welcome to JamBox!

JamBox is a creation of mine that I have done purely for fun. It offers nothing that Spotify or other webscraping sites don't already do.

Key Features

  • Top Songs Over Time: Will show your top most songs for given periods.
  • Recently Played: Stay up-to-date with your most recent musical adventures.
  • Soon coming - classify your taste as a barbie: Don't ask how I am going to do this, but at one point, I will develop this website to analyse your music profile and then classify you as a barbie character. This will provide you with great insights that will most certainly be relevant to your day to day life (trust trust)
